# Racoon Rumpus

This game was created in 72 hours for Ludum Dare 58.

Take on the role of a raccoon trying to scavenge precious trash in the harsh environment of a suburban backyard. Collect as much trash as you can as fast as possible, but be wary of the neighborhood dogs, trying to protect their ownersâ€™ property. If you are found hide in the bushes to get to safety!

## Controls
WASD to Move, F to interact with trash cans, Space to jump.
